Counselors' Corner

Jefferson County Public Schools (JCPS) counselors, in partnership with students, families, staff, community members, and businesses, will prepare students to be successful lifelong learners. The grade-nine through grade-twelve Comprehensive Development Guidance Program will provide teaching and modeling strategies that create and nurture a safe and positive teaching environment.

This program also provides the skills necessary for academic success, career planning, and personal and social growth.
