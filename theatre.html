On March 7, PRP held a dedication ceremony for the new performing arts building. Community leaders and PRP students and staff all participated in the event. The ceremony began with Assistant Superintendent Brian Schumate speaking and thanking everyone involved in the construction of the new building. Other speakers included Congressman John Yarmuth, Superintendent Donna Hargens, Board Member Chuck Haddaway, Councilman Rick Blackwell, and Mayor Greg Fischer.

Mr. Gilbert (below left) has been involved with the PRP Drama department for many years. His work has helped produce numerous successful plays here at PRP. We are excited that he will be involved with our new facility!

Mr. Braun (below right) is involved with the PRP Preforming Arts program. He has directed numerous outstanding plays and is continuing to make more with the help of the students and staff.

"It is the best of the best, state-of-the art theatre. It has the Cadillac of all sound systems." -Mr. Gilbert

"Was long overdue and much needed here at PRP. Holds 350 people and is a fantastic addition to not just PRP but the community." -Mr. Braun
