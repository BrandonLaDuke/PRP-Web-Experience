Athletics

Bowling

Since its inception in the year 2000, the Panther Bowling Team has walked away victoriously in either their conference, regional, or State Championships (or all three) every year. Everyone gets to participate at some time during the matches and in practice. The students seem to enjoy doing all the activities that we participate in and have tremendous team spirit that has carried down year after year. We have won the State Championship with our girls in 2004, 2007, 2008, 2010, and 2011. The boys were State Champions in 2006.
